---
title: 'Create Local Fees'
---

1. Link or create a Stripe account (Sidebar -> Resources -> Local group settings)

![Image](/images/platform/Screenshot-2025-03-26-at-11.27.39.png)

![Image](/images/platform/Screenshot-2025-03-26-at-11.26.52.png)

2. Create a Fee/Membership product under 'Fees settings'

![Image](/images/platform/Screenshot-2025-03-26-at-11.29.57.png)

3. Configure your product's settings

![Image](/images/platform/Screenshot-2025-03-26-at-11.31.48.png)

4. Add prices (membership tiers) to your product

![Image](/images/platform/Screenshot-2025-03-26-at-11.33.42.png)

5. Back to Local group settings, under Fees settings you will notice the the product and default membership tier was set. You can change them at anytime using the dropdown selector

![Image](/images/platform/Screenshot-2025-03-26-at-11.35.26.png)
